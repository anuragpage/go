The Instructor

Anurag Sharma

Government-certified yoga instructor, practising since 2001 and teaching since 2011. Trained across leading institutions including Art of Living, Vipassana Centre, Patanjali, Chandigarh College of Yoga, and Chandigarh Administration.

Has taught in government schools and private colleges in Kharar and Mohali, and conducted weekly sessions at hospitals in Chandigarh including PGI's drug & alcohol de-addiction centre.

Every year serves as Master Yoga Trainer for Chandigarh Administration, preparing participants for the International Yoga Day event. Currently running regular sessions in residential societies of Kharar and nearby areas.

Also teaches Meditation, Pranayama, Jal Neti and other shatkarmas.
